Written By Mia G, Illustrated by Riccardo

Once upon a time, in a land far away,
there lived a girl with a red ribbon in her hair.
In the sun, it shone a bright red like Ruby Slippers.
And in the moonlight, a beautiful maroon.
With it, the girl hid a secret of her own.


Bound by gold ties and antique book binds.
Once it's read, it takes you elsewhere.
To a land where adventure, wonder, and excitement strive.
A place of magic comparable to the Philosopher's Stone.


Fairy tales of noble knights and deals with a price.
Of fairy godmothers and star-crossed lovers.
Of gingerbread houses and looking glasses.
Oh, what a tale!


Once, when you wish upon a star,
What you long for is not afar.
When you leave the world behind,
Hidden treasures you may find.


Talking teacups and magic carpets.
Glass slippers and wooden puppets.
Hidden doors and little notes.
Oh, what a tale!


Magic comes to life in these tales.
Serendipitous moments beyond compare.
Chance encounters where fate is true,
Destiny follows through.


In real life, serendipitous moments may be hard to find.
Life is not as clear as within these stories,
Yet, if you look closely, you may hear the euphonies.
A tale as old as time.


A gentle touch to a heartful hug,
The morning sunrise to a starry night,
A stranger's smile to a shared interest,
Spring showers to harmonious rainbows.


Fortunate mistakes that lead to moments of joy.
For in those moments, fairy tale dreams do come true.
"Fairfarren, Alice," said the Mad Hatter,
And so she lived happily ever after.
