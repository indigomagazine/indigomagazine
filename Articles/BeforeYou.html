Before You and After You

Written By Saee Joshi

There is before you,
and there is after you.
You touched my life,
and then you left.
You were here,
and then you werenâ€™t.

Do you know?
Do you know how you changed me
in the few moments you were mine?
Do you know
that my life is measured in
before you and after you?

There is no during you,
because you slipped through
my fingers far too soon.
I cling to the idea of you
as tightly as I once clung to you.

We weren't ships
passing in the night.
Such a comparison is inadequate
for the waves you caused.
Maybe you were the iceberg,
and me, the ship,
my entire course thrown off
by one touch from you.

I wonder,
is your life measured in
before me and after me?
Do I live in your mind
as you live in mine?

Sometimes I wish
there was no before you.
Sometimes I wish
that we were parallel lines,
together for as long as we go on.

But there was a before you.
And your path crossed mine
for a mere second.
A gasp in the breaths of a lifespan.
And now I must live forevermore

in the after you.

How serendipitous and cruel our fate is.
