Medusa

Written by Caden S. & Design by Silas S.

I am a sigil of protection.
I am carved into doorways to ward off evil and painted on floors to keep out intruders.
I am stitched into clothing, singed into pottery, crafted into currency, all to prevent ill
will and catastrophes.

I am an emblem of fear.
I am the mask of the most menacing warrior, the shield of the vengeful gods,
battle-ready and blood-splattered.
I fill the hearts of men with dread, just at the sight of me.


I am a symbol of survival.
I have been inked into the flesh of women who did not stop fighting and endured.
I am a woman violated once, but never touched again.


I am tusks, bulging eyes, sharp teeth, and forked tongue,
Hideous and fearsome, from a face once coveted.
I am a promise of certain death.
I am the Gorgoneion.
I am Medusa.


Run.
