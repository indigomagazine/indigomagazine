Indigo Magazine presents Serendipity: an exploration of moments that make life beautiful.

Sometimes the stars align. Sometimes the right thing happens at the right time. Sometimes our paths cross in the sweetest way. Sometimes two people find each other exactly when they need each other the most. It is never permanent. No, permanence would take away the beauty of the moment. But for the briefest moment, we find our hearts smiling because the universe graced us in a way only it can. We find ourselves basking in the warmth of good fortune. We find ourselves mumbling, “I’m glad I got to know you.” If lifetimes were measured in lines, these moments would be mere dots. But these moments, as slippery as they may be, remind us how rare and beautiful it is to even exist. Indigo Magazine presents Serendipity: an exploration of moments that make life beautiful.
