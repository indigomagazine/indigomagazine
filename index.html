INDIGO MAGAZINE IS FEELING....FEAR.

Fear. It's one of the most human emotions to exist. We face it everyday. It's in the first day of school, your first date, your first driving lesson. It's in moving across the country from your family for your dream job. It's in the relationship you think is ending. It's in the devastating diagnosis you just received. Sometimes it lingers in your bones. Sometimes it whispers in your ear and sometimes, it sets your nerves on fire.

Fear molds our actions more than we realize. Fight or flight. Stay or run. Freeze or react. For our latest issue, Indigo Magazine presents Fear: An exploration of how one emotion can shape humanity.
