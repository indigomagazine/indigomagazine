Angels All Along

Written By Manjiri

I rue the day this will all disappear,
I spent centuries
Concerned over an unknown ending
Over my sins seen by juries
Of those I love and those I loved
but grew to hate. 


Even the happiest moments
Gone into ashes
Scattered into a river 
Dissipating to no end.
But I didn’t expect this reality
I didn’t expect-


I didn’t expect to wake up


To a million eyes surrounding me
They sang in harmony like a singular entity
But, this time, without judgement


A feather for every lapse in my own
Stuck in my spine as a reminder


Oh, wouldn’t it have been nice to know!

Warnings of a luciferous descent
Raged at me through earthly tales
But all I see when I look down


Is the soil where the gavel sealed my fate.
The existence of an inferno
Which I carried like a burden,
Like an oath
Was nowhere to be found
